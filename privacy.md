# Eon — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 3, 2026_

**Eon collects no data.**

- **No analytics, no tracking, no ads.** The app contains no analytics or
  advertising SDKs and sends nothing to the developer.
- **Your Life Time credentials.** You sign in on Life Time's own login page.
  Your password is entered directly with Life Time and is never visible to
  Eon. The resulting session token is stored only in your device's secure
  Keychain and is used solely to make requests to Life Time's services on
  your behalf.
- **Location.** If you grant location access, it is used on your device only
  to sort Life Time clubs by distance. It is never transmitted off the
  device.
- **Network traffic.** The app communicates only with Life Time's own
  services (to load schedules and make/cancel the reservations you request)
  and with Apple's standard system services.
- **Notifications.** Waitlist and reservation alerts are generated on your
  device.

Because Eon collects nothing, there is nothing for the developer to sell,
share, or delete. Signing out removes the stored session from your device.
