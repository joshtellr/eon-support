# Eon — Support

Eon is an iPhone app for browsing and booking **swim lanes and studio
classes at Life Time clubs**, using your own Life Time account.

## Get help

Email **joshuateller@icloud.com** with any question, bug report, or feature
request. Please include your device, iOS version, and what you were doing
when the issue occurred.

## Common questions

**Do I need a Life Time membership?**
Yes. Eon requires an active Life Time membership. You sign in with your own
Life Time account and Eon books through it, exactly as if you had booked on
Life Time's own site or app.

**Is my password safe?**
You sign in on Life Time's own login page, presented inside the app. Your
password goes only to Life Time — Eon never sees it or stores it. After you
sign in, Eon keeps a session token in your device's secure Keychain.

**Why does the app ask for my location?**
Only to sort nearby clubs to the top of the club picker. Your location is
used on-device and never transmitted anywhere.

**How do I sign out?**
Account tab → Session → Sign out. This removes the stored session from your
device.
